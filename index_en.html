Celeste

Celeste is a 2D platformer game created by the Canadian developer Extreme OK Games, with the visionary mind behind it being Maddy from Maddy Makes Games. Celeste was released on January 25, 2018. The game's protagonist is Madeline, a young woman who, facing challenges in her life, decides to climb a mountain that is revealed to be magical.

  Gameplay

The game features fast and precise movement, yet with great fluidity, making it easy to learn but challenging to master. Celeste follows a linear progression, both in terms of chapters and difficulty, introducing new mechanics gradually, allowing players to become more accustomed to the game.

The game is divided into 9 chapters, in addition to the prologue and epilogue.

  Developers:

Celeste was originally developed in 4 days as a Pico-8 game for a Game Jam. This game, after the release of the complete Celeste, is referenced as Celeste Classic. The original Pico-8 game was developed by Maddy Thorson and Noel Berry.

After the official game release in 2018 under the developer's name Maddy Makes Games, Maddy and a group of friends formed Extremely OK Games, which assisted in updates for Maddy's games, as well as a new project set to be launched in 2024.
