Celeste chapters

Celeste has a total of 9 chapters, each with its own mechanics and level design.

The base game has a prologue, 7 chapters and an epiloogue.

Chapter 8 was added in the DLC Core.

Chapter 9 foi was added in the DLC Farewell.

All A-Side and B-Side chapters (except for the prologue and epilogue) have subchapters that divide them.

To unlock an A-Side chapter, the player must finish the previous chapter (except the prologue).

To unlock an B-Side chapter, the player must to acquire the cassette tape of the respective chapter.

To unlock an C-Side chapter, the player must finish the B-Side of the respective chapter.

All the chapters have a crystal heart, which are used to pass trough barriers in chapter 8 A-Side (4 hearts), chapter 8 B-Side (15 heart), chapter 8 C-Side (23 hearts) and chapter 9 (15 hearts). Futhermore, all chapters also have a golden strawberry.

All A-Side chapters have strawberries and a cassette tape (used to unlock B-Side).

B-Sides always end with a final room with rhythm block.
