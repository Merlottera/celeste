Characters

Celeste features significant character development, prompting players to empathize and feel immersed in the game's universe. It's also possible to notice the intention of fostering a sense of identification between the player and the characters. To achieve this, the characters are portrayed as flawed, human figures. As a result, the game establishes emotional bonds (albeit fictional) between the player and the characters.

It is possible to find information about some characters on Instagram at TheoUnderStars. Created by the developers, it references Theo's InstaPix in the game.
