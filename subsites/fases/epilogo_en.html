Epilogue

The epilogue is the phase unlocked after chapter 7 of Celeste. It is the easiest phase in the game.

  Plot

After Madeline (and Badeline) descends from the Summit, they go to the Old Woman's house (as indicated in the cutscene, a bird suggested they visit the house). Theo, the old woman, and Madeline are all in the house when Mr. Oshiro enters. Later, Madeline bakes a strawberry pie, and the reaction (and final photo) depend on the number of strawberries the player has collected.

  Endings

If you collected 0-19 strawberries: Blank expressions from everyone, Badeline doesn't speak and appears disappointed with a simple pie. Theo accepts the pie, Mr. Oshiro complains, and the Old Woman says strawberry pies should have strawberries. If you collected 20-49 strawberries: Everyone more accepting of the pie, now with a strawberry and red filling. Theo declines, Badeline complains, saying she'll eat the pie alone. If you collected 50-89 strawberries: Everyone more satisfied with a visible strawberry on top of the pie. Congratulations to Madeline, Badeline unimpressed with Mr. Oshiro. If you collected 90-149 strawberries: Two strawberries on top, everyone happier. Old Woman praises Madeline, Badeline impressed, Theo surprised, and Mr. Oshiro invites Madeline to his banquet. If you collected 150 or more strawberries: Three strawberries, strawberry ice cream, wings on the strawberry, and whipped cream on the crust. Theo loves the pie, Madeline doubts, Old Woman agrees, Mr. Oshiro finds it magnificent, and Badeline wants credit, with a strawberry in her hair.
