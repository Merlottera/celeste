Capítulo 8: Núcleo

The Core is the eighth chapter of Celeste. This chapter introduces a new unique mechanic: the dash is no longer recharged when the player steps on a platform or the ground, and it can be recovered after passing through a room transition or by picking up a dash crystal.

  Plot

After plunging into the abyss, Madeline and Badeline embark on an ascent up the mountain once again. They traverse pint-sized renditions of every preceding chapter, confronting numerous familiar obstacles embedded in those stages. Nevertheless, the conclusive segment diverges from each preceding sub-chapter.

  Collectibles

Crystal Heart:

To obtain the crystal heart in Chapter 7, you must locate six Jewels hidden in each subchapter—a blue star for Reflection at the Start, a sapphire square for Forsaken City at 500 M, an emerald oval for Old Site at 1000 M, a gold circle for Celestial Resort at 1500 M, a pink diamond for Golden Ridge at 2000 M, and a white pearl for Mirror Temple at 2500 M. You acquire the crystal heart at 3000 M after collecting all the Jewels.

Cassette:

Chapter 7's cassette tape is acquired in the fourth sub-chapter in a secret room.

Strawberries:

• Golden strawberry:
