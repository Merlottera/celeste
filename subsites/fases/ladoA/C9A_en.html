Chapter 9: Farewell

Farewell is the ninth and final chapter of Celeste. It is considered the most challenging phase of the game, requiring a high level of skill to complete.

  Mechanics

Farewell introduces 4 new mechanics:

• Pufferfish,

• Jellyfish Parachutes,

• Energy,

• Energy Boxes,

  Crystal Heart Barrier

  Plot

The chapter starts with Madeline and Theo taking a break near a campfire. Madeline reveals her struggles against a haunted part of herself. She thanks Theo for his support and shares her battles with depression. In a dream, Madeline tries to free the troublesome part but faces resistance. She falls into a deep abyss, landing in a crystal cave, feeling hopeless. Upon meeting the Old Woman, she learns to cooperate with her inner part, Badeline. After a boss fight, they merge into one, gaining a new ability. Climbing again, they decide to face the summit together, emphasizing the power of cooperation.

  Collectibles

Crystal Heart:

Chapter 9 has only one fake crystal heart. It is found in the fourth subchapter, required to continue progressing.

Strawberries:

• Golden Strawberry:

Obtained after completing the chapter while holding the golden strawberry. Can only be obtained after completing Chapter 8's B-side.

• Moon Berry:

Exclusive strawberry of this chapter. Obtained in the last subchapter after turning off the energy and making the way to the left, going back the entire map until reaching a secret room with a final challenge preceding the Moon Berry.
