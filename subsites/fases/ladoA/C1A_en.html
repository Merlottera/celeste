Chapter 1: Forsaken City

Forsaken City is the first chapter of Celeste. The chapter has an easy level design so that the player gets used to the game mechanics.

- See also: Chapter 1: B-Side

- See also: Chapter 1: C-Side

  Mechanics

Forsaken City introduces 6 new mechanics:

• Spikes,

• Springs,

• Traffic Light Platforms,

• Disappearing Platforms,

• Breakable Walls,

• Dash Crystal.

  Plot

Cidade Abandonada introduces 1 new character:

• Theo.

Madeline journeys through the Forsaken City, discovering another adventurer named Theo midway. They engage in conversation, where Theo shares his purpose of exploring Celeste Mountain and capturing photos. However, Madeline remains elusive about her own motives for being on the mountain. The two delve into the history of the Forsaken City, with Madeline explaining that a mega-corporation initiated its construction, but it was abandoned due to a lack of inhabitants, though the reason remains unknown. Madeline expresses her aspiration to reach the summit, a goal that doesn't seem to resonate with Theo. After a while, Madeline resumes her ascent of Celeste Mountain. Upon reaching the level's conclusion, she encounters a memorial called the Celeste Memorial, decides to camp there for the night, and goes to sleep, questioning whether climbing Celeste Mountain was a mistake.

  Collectibles

Crystal Heart:

The crystal heart in Chapter 1 is obtained in the second sub-chapter after accessing a secret passage. In the crystal heart room, there is a computer that flashes colored lights in a sequence, and some birds of the same colors as the computer move in different directions. To obtain the crystal heart, you need to dash in the direction of the birds in the order shown by the computer. The correct order is:

• Up (White),
• Left (Purple),
• Down-right (Blue),
• Up-right (Red),
• Left (Purple),
• Up-left (Yellow).

Cassette:

The cassette tape in Chapter 1 is obtained in the third sub-chapter after accessing a secret passage. In the cassette tape room, you'll encounter rhythmic platforms. After reaching the objective, a bubble will transport the player back to the beginning of the room.

Strawberries:

• Golden Strawberry:

Obtained after completing the chapter while holding the golden strawberry. It can only be achieved after completing chapter 8 - B-Side.

• Winged golden strawberry:

Obtained after completing Chapter 1 without using the dash at all (the player may use it by accident, but if you move to another room after using the dash and haven't died, the strawberry will disappear).
