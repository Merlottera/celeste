Chapter 5: Mirror Temple - B-Side

Mirror temple is the fifth chapter of Celeste. B-Side features a significant increase in difficulty. To unlock, the player must get the cassette tape on A-Side of the respective chapter.

The crystal heart of chapter 5 - B-Side is acquired after completing the chapter.

Obtained after completing the chapter while holding the golden strawberry. It can only be achieved after completing chapter 8 - B-Side.
