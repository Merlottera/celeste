Chapter 8: Core - C-Side

Core is the eighth chapter of Celeste. B side features a significant increase in difficulty. To unlock, the player must complete the A-Side of the respective chapter.

At the beginning of the stage, there is a crystal heart barrier, and to pass through, 23 crystal hearts are required.

Completing the chapter will unlock the variants.
