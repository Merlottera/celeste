Prologue

The prologue is the first stage of Celeste. It's the easiest level in the game.

  Mechanics

The prologue teaches the player the most basic game mechanics, introducing:

• Falling plataforms,

• Climbing,

• Collapsing bridge,

• Dash.

  Plot

The prologue introduces 3 new characters:

• Madeline,

• Granny,

• Bird.

The prologue begins with Madeline mentally encouraging herself to continue her journey. Subsequently, Madeline encounters a bird that teaches her how to climb. Afterward, Madeline meets an elderly woman and asks for directions. The elderly woman mocks Madeline and warns her about the dangers of the mountain, but Madeline dismisses her as crazy and continues on her path. Shortly after, Madeline crosses a bridge that collapses as she traverses it, causing her to start falling. However, the bird teaches Madeline about the dash, saving her. Madeline reaches the ground and encourages herself once again, thus completing the stage.

  Cheat Mode

Cheat Mode can be accessed from the prologue. To unlock Cheat Mode, from the starting room, take the left path. Inside this room, only one car is found. To activate Cheat Mode, a sequence of commands is required: Left - Right - Journal - Grab - Up - Up - Down - Left - Grab - Confirm. After this, the player will return to the main page, with access to all chapters, and the game profile will gain a "Cheat Mode" seal.
